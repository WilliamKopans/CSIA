About Us

Founded in 2007 through the generous donation of a Colby alumnus, the Colby Student Investment Association (CSIA) is a student-run club which manages an equity portfolio of over $100k. Relying on in-house original research we aim to outperform the market indicies and provide invaluable financial literacy education to club members. Our guiding principle revolves around nurturing a strong connection between Colby students and alumni in the financial services sector. We hold long positions across industries and focus on investing in companies with a strong focus on ESG initiatives. Each year a portion of the portfolio’s earnings is contributed to the Colby College Endowment.
